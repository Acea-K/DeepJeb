# DLC Knowledge

## Making History DLC
- New parts: 1.875m, 5m sizes; Soviet-style parts; historical mission parts
- **Mission Builder:** Create custom scenarios with objectives, triggers, dialogs
- **Launch Sites:** Desert and beach launch sites (enable in difficulty settings)
- **Suits:** Retro-style spacesuits

## Breaking Ground DLC

**Deployed Science:** Place science equipment on surfaces via EVA
- 4 types: weather analyzer, ion detector, seismometer, Goo Observation Monitor

**Robotic Parts:** Pistons, rotors, hinges, servos
- Controlled via action groups and axis groups
- Torque limit affects power draw and mass

**Propellers & Rotors:**
- Use axial symmetry (not mirror), press R in editor to switch
- Blade yellow side faces direction of travel
- Counter-torque: coaxial rotors, tail rotor, or counter-rotating props

**Surface Features:** Scatter (rocks, crystals) with collision

**RGB Suits:** New astronaut suits with RGB lighting

### Robotic Part Parameters
| Parameter | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Target Extend/Target Angle | Default position/angle (editor) or target (flight) |
| Traverse Rate | Movement speed |
| Damping | Lower = faster response, higher = smoother |
| Motorized | Whether part has powered movement |
| Torque Limit | Maximum torque output (higher = more mass & power) |
