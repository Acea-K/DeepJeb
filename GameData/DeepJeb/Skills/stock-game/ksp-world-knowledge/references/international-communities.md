# International Communities

## Japanese Community
- **KSP Wiki (日本語版):** https://wikiwiki.jp/ksp/
- Detailed pages on orbital mechanics and aircraft design
- Active on Twitter/X and Niconico

## Russian Community
- **KSP Wiki (RU):** https://wiki.kerbalspaceprogram.com/wiki/Main_Page/ru
- **KSP.SPACE:** https://ksp.space/ — news, guides, crafts, mods
- **SpaceDock.ru:** https://spacedock.ru/ — active forum (230+ gameplay topics, 194+ mod topics)
- One of the most active non-English KSP communities
