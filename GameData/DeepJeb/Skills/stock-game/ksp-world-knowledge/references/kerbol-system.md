# The Kerbol System

## The Sun (Kerbol)
Central star. Cannot be landed on. SOI extremely large.

| Body | Radius (km) | Surface g (m/s²) | Atmosphere | Notes |
|------|-------------|------------------|------------|-------|
| **Kerbin** | 600 | 9.81 (1.0g) | 70 km, O₂ present | Escape v: 3,431 m/s. LKO (80 km): ~2,280 m/s. Rotation: 6h. Δv to orbit: 3,200–3,400 m/s |
